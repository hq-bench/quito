About QuitoBench

Time series foundation models (FMs) promise universal forecasting, yet evaluations remain confined to small-scale academic benchmarks. QUITO is a high-quality, billion-scale CloudOps time series corpus from real-world Alipay production systems. Built atop QUITO, QuitoBench benchmarks 10 models — spanning deep learning, foundation models, and statistical baselines — across 232,200 evaluation instances organized into 8 balanced TSF regimes.
